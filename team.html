ggplot(data = dat1, aes(x=happy, y=age)) + geom_boxplot() + theme(axis.text.x=element_text(angle=90,hjust=1,vjust=0.5)) + facet_wrap(vars(health)) +xlab("Happiness") + ylab("Age") + labs(title="Boxplot of Age by happiness, faceted by Health")

The relationship between and age seems to not actually matter the median age of all happiness levels are about the same there isnt one group that is happier than another. It interesting to note that as median age increases the health condition decreases. The most interesting thing is that when people respond that they dont know what their health is like then there is a relationship between age and happiness. Older peole tend to be happier when they dont know their health condition. It seems to be that if you want to stay happy when you get old stop worrying about your health.
